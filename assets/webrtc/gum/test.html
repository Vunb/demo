simpl.info getUserMedia constraints

This example uses constraints.

Click a button to call getUserMedia() with appropriate resolution.

GetUserMedia constraints are matched with a fixed list of resolutions independent on what the camera actually support.
The list is fixed and used on all platforms.
  • 1280x720
  • 960x720
  • 640x360
  • 640x480
  • 320x240
  • 320x180

For more information, see Capturing Audio & Video in HTML5 on HTML5 Rocks.
